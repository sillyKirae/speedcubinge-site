Speedcubing

Impressive, right ?

Well here's how you can solve it!

  1. First, learn what the name of the pieces are; Corner, Centers, Edge.
  2. Then, make a white cross on the white side, making sure all the colors are aligned.
  3. Secondly, make the first layer by putting the corners in the good place and doing a simple algorithm.
  4. Of course, you'll do the same for the second layer with another algorithm.
    5. After this step, if you see that one the cases are not covered in the video you WILL need to disassemble and reassemble your cube, as it is unsovable.
  5. Fourthly, you're going to make a yellow cross on the top, using the different patterns.
  6. Consequently, you will orientate the edges of the top face.
  7. following this logic, you will orientate the corners of the top face.
  8. Finally, solve the cube with one algorithm!
